Murder of Nora Allen

The murder of Nora Allen was a tragic event that killed Barry Allen. At the time of the incident, young Barry was not believed. The police thought his dad commited the murder and he was seen as crazy.

Before

There is not much known about Barry before this, but his father was a doctor and his mother's job is not known. There was no sign of Henry Allen acting bizarre

During

Barry Allen saw a flash of light and he was very surprised to see this happen. He later described this event as "something impossible". This was very disturbing to Barry Allen, especially considering the fact that his dad was a doctor.

After

Barry Allen was taken with Joe West, who had a daughter named Iris. Although Joe arrested him, he said to Barry that he thinks he was right and that he was killed by someone else.
