STAR Labs

STAR Labs is a government contractor that studies the collision of subatomic particles for military purposes. This institution has done public events in the past, like turning on the particle accelerator. It is ran by Harrison Wells, a scientist that allowed the explosion to happen because it was not really Harrison Wells, but the Reverse Flash.

Before the particle accelerator explosion

STAR Labs was founded in 1904 by Thomas Edison as a division of the Accelerati as a secret organization. In 1917, Edison began relocation efforts to transfer control to another scientist. Some of the substances were kept under Accelerati control. In 1919, Albert Einstein was selected. He kept the secret of where it came from. Calculating the best way to conceal the equipment required the best analog computers and smallest safe computations possible. Ernest Rutherford was invited as the assistant director of what was then known as the Star Division of the Accelerati. The motto was "studying the makeup of the stars and the Earth". Rutherford was selected because Rutherford discovered that atoms had protons, which the Accelerati put to special use to create and study radioisotopes beginning in 1932.
After the Accelerati's division from STAR Labs happened, STAR Labs announced it existed in 1929. Because they no longer had access to the material that they had when the Accelerati was in charge, they had financing problems because science institutions had a hard time believing that they were what they thought they were. In 1948, after Hitler was defeated, the First Technological Surge began. 5 million people around the world were voluntarily hired to research mathematics and the spacetime continnum. These people were able to stay up for long periods of time and some of the people were not exactly normal but very smart. STAR Labs benefitted from this and after the International Unification in 1956, which put all of the countries into the same position. When this happened, 74% of the member states voted to unify the world under the United Territories of Earth One.
STAR Labs as a whole did not know about the metahumans. But, Harrison Wells knew about metahumans. This is because he traveled back in time to kill the Flash's mother because he wanted to try to stop him despite the fact that there may be a time paradox, a factor that he considered but determined was worth the risk. That is why he ignored all of the warnings and continued to build the particle accelerator despite the fact that there may be dark matter released.

During and shortly after the explosion

Five seconds before the explosion, an alarm went off. This is because in STAR Labs, they were having a hard time controlling the situation. Despite their great minds, they could not stop it. But, this was the beginning of something new. The Flash was created by STAR Labs.
STAR Labs handled the situation by allowing and encouraging Barry Allen to excercise his power and encourage him to do so. This is because they saw the good in his superhero strength.
