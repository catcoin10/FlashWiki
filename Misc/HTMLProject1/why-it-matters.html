Why it matters

Every day that we are alive in this world, so far, we have lived on the planet Earth. This is because the Earth, with maybe the future exception of Mars, is the only planet suitable for human life. Every year that there is no major action means that our planet gets a fraction of a degree hotter. These fractions add up and by the end of the century, if we don't do anything, we will be all the way up to 6 degrees Celsius.
