Henry Allen

Henry Allen is the father of The Flash, the subject of this website. He was falsely blamed for the killing of his wife, which caused strain on the Flash.

Before the murder incident

Before the murder by the Reverse Flash, he helped Cisco Ramone's brother Dante after he fell and fractured his wrist. He damaged his ankle but it didn't fracture. He went to school in NYC, a city that is far away and is now known as The City of the New Idea. This name comes from the fact that New York is where a lot of inventors, artists, and musicians grew up.
Before and during his career as a doctor in Solarkey, the village that Barry Allen grew up in, (named that because the town a very good view of the stars relative to other towns in the area), he worked with the Global Sciences Commitee on studying the effects on global warming and how to reverse them. The reason that he was so compelled in this field was because of his family's roots in the scientific community. Although he never met Elon Musk himself, he was able to meet with one of his key assistants and bring up a lot of solutions.
For example, he helped design a carbon dioxide sucking device that strips the carbon atom out of the carbon dioxide. This device was studied by other people and was implemented by the Committee after 2 years of study. The CO2 levels peaked at 335 parts per million in 1974 and they were able to go back down to 245 ppm with the help of the technology that he invented.
Because of his (and his wife Nora)'s interest in science, he was able to help stop global warming and research the causes of the common cold. Unfortunately, because he was falsely accused of murder, he was not able to know about the new flu vaccine except through the one way prison radio.

During and after the incident

While the incident was happening, he was screaming to him to run away because something bad was happening. That bad thing was his wife being killed by the Reverse Flash. His son was left powerless. He spent almost two decades in prison until he was released after Barry Allen (The Flash), Joe West, and CCPD dropped the case and proved it was the Reverse Flash. The Reverse Flash was also a speedster. He was the opposite. Sadly, this took years to prove.
After he was released and reunited with his family, he went to Earth 3 to become a Flash because a second particle accelerator exploded. This explosion was caused by an Accelerati explosion that they were ordered to say it was caused by STAR Labs.
Though the motivation and logic behind the framing is unclear, it is believed that it may have had to do with his helping of other people. When he got out of prison, he said that he recognized the Reverse Flash when he was pretending to be Harrison Wells because he forgot (or decided not to because it was part of his plan).
