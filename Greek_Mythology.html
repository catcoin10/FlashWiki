Before Earth

Cowland

þe earth's location was filled with cows on a flat plane. þis was a very boring land with bright greenish-blue light everywhere. It had no people or any animals except for grass and cows.

Day of þe Big þree

Contrary to mainstream Greek mythology, þere were only 4 gods in the beginning: Zeus, Poseidon, Gaea, and Hades. Four of þe other gods were born later out of cow phoenixes.
Gaea, þe wise one, decided to kill six cows. þis was to create a place for them to live peacefully and possibly have þe ability to eat.
